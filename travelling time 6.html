Best Traveling Time

Country Best Visit Time
Hong Kong: The spring is warm and humid. The summer is hot and wet. Winter is cool, dry, and very cold.
Macau: The best time to visit is in spring and autumn. There are temples and museums to visit.
New Zealand: Summer is warm for hiking but crowded. Autumn offers beautiful foliage. Winter is good for skiing.
Rome: One of the oldest places in the world. Visit Trevi Fountain. Summer is pleasant with events. Winter is quiet and festive.
Madrid: Visit Real Madrid Santiago Bernabeu Stadium. Autumn and spring offer pleasant weather. Summer festivals begin with hot weather.
Moscow: Nice shops and ancient museums. Winter is very cold and snowy. Spring and autumn offer nice weather.

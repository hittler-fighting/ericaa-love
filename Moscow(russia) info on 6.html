Basic Info

Moscow, the capital of Russia, is a city steeped in history and culture. It's a sprawling metropolis with a population of over 13 million people.

  • Kremlin: The seat of Russian political power for centuries, the Kremlin is a must-see for any visitor to Moscow. This fortified complex encompasses palaces, cathedrals, and government buildings.
  • Red Square: Located adjacent to the Kremlin, Red Square is a symbolic heart of Russia. Witness the iconic landmarks like St. Basil's Cathedral, Lenin's Mausoleum, and GUM department store.
Fun and Interesting Things to Do in Moscow (Russia)

Moscow offers a wealth of attractions and experiences beyond the typical tourist spots. Here are some exciting options to explore:

  • Moscow by Night: Witness the city transformed with a nighttime river cruise or a walking tour focused on illuminated sights like the Bolshoi Theatre and Red Square.
  • Metro Museum and Depository Tour: Delve into the hidden history of the Moscow Metro, known for its opulent stations. Explore restricted areas and see vintage metro cars.

Basic Info

Macao, also spelled Macau, is a special administrative region (SAR) of China located on the southern coast of the country, across the Pearl River Delta from Hong Kong. It was a Portuguese colony from the mid-16th century until 1999, when it was returned to China. As a result, Macao has a unique blend of Chinese and Portuguese cultures, which is evident in its architecture, cuisine, and language.

  • Casinos: Macao is often called the "Las Vegas of Asia" because of its many casinos.
Fun and Interesting Things to Do in Macao

Macao offers a unique blend of history, culture, and entertainment, making it a great destination for a fun vacation. Here are some interesting and fun things you can do while visiting Macao:

  • Experience the Cotai Strip: Nicknamed the "Las Vegas of Asia", the Cotai Strip is a must-visit for anyone wanting to experience the glitz and glam of Macao's casinos.
